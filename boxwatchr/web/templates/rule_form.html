Conditions

For an address like user@mail.example.com:

  • Full address: the entire address (user@mail.example.com)
  • Username: everything before the @ (user)
  • Full domain: everything after the @ (mail.example.com)
  • Subdomain + domain: excludes the TLD (mail.example)
  • Domain (no subdomain): the domain only, no subdomain or TLD (example)
  • TLD: top-level domain only (com)

Username, Subdomain + domain, and Domain (no subdomain) ignore punctuation when matching — hyphens, dots, and other non-alphanumeric characters are stripped from both the address and your value before comparing. Full address, Full domain, and TLD match the exact text, so your value must include any hyphens or dots as they appear.
